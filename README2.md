# Virus The Game - Rules

## Objective
Be the first player to have **4 healthy organs** laid out in front of you.

An organ counts as healthy if it is:
- **Untouched**,
- **Vaccinated**, or
- **Immune** (has received two vaccines)

Dead organs do **not** count and are removed from play.

---

## Game Components

### Cards
The deck contains the following types of cards:

#### Organ Cards
- Colors: red, green, blue, yellow
- Special: rainbow organ (counts as any color)
- Used to build your body

#### Virus Cards
- Same colors as organs (plus rainbow)
- Used to infect organs

#### Vaccine Cards
- Same colors as organs (plus rainbow)
- Used to heal or protect organs

#### Special Cards
- Cards that break or modify normal rules (e.g. transplant, organ thief, etc.)

---

## Setup
1. Shuffle the deck.
2. Deal **3 cards** to each player.
3. Players keep their hands secret.
4. A starting player is the youngest one or the winner of the previous round.

---

## Turn Structure
On your turn:

1. **Play one card** from your hand, or **discard any number of cards** from your hand
2. **Draw cards** until you have **3 cards** in hand.

You may not exceed 3 cards in hand at the end of your turn.

---

## Playing Cards

### Playing an Organ
- Place the organ face-up in front of you.
- You may only have **one organ of each color**.
- Rainbow organs can act as any color, but still count as only one organ.

---

### Playing a Virus
- A virus must match the **color** of the target organ (or be rainbow). You can place a virus of any color on a rainbow organ.
- You may target:
  - Your own organs
  - Another player’s organs

**Effects:**
- Healthy organ → **Sick**
- Sick organ → **Dead** (organ is removed from play)
- Vaccinated organ → Vaccine is removed instead
- Immune organ → No effect

---

### Playing a Vaccine
- A vaccine must match the **color** of the target organ (or be rainbow). You can place a vaccine of any color on a rainbow organ.
- You may only target your own organs.

**Effects:**
- Sick organ → **Healthy**
- Healthy organ → **Vaccinated**
- Vaccinated organ → **Immune**
- Immune organ → No effect

---

## Organ States Summary

| State        | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Healthy     | No virus, no vaccine |
| Sick        | One virus attached |
| Vaccinated  | One vaccine attached |
| Immune      | Two vaccines attached |
| Dead        | Two viruses attached (removed from play) |

---

## Special Cards
Special cards allow actions such as:
- Swapping organs between players
- Stealing organs
- Removing all viruses/vaccines from an organ
- Blocking other special cards

Special cards override normal rules as specified on the card.

---

## Winning the Game
A player wins immediately when they have **4 healthy or immune organs** laid out.

Dead organs do not count and are removed as soon as they die.

---

## Notes
- Players are never required to play a card on their turn.
- All effects are resolved immediately.
- If multiple rules conflict, **special card rules take priority**.
